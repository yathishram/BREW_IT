About Brew It!

Well it is not just a simple Web lab project for us. It is something close to our hearts. Coffee is something we have found to be intriguing and unknown to us and eventually ending up as love. We tried our level best to tell a story of coffee with this project. This website aims to get people thinking about what exactly is going on in India with coffee. Any person visiting this website can goof around to learn about different brewing methods, different places to buy the best coffee. It was designed to be minimal and concentrate more on the Usability. Well there is a lot of work to be done in order to improve some functionalities which shall be added in the future. Until then Keep Calm Drink Coffee.
